Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Gozar Records collects, uses, and handles information in connection with LuchBot, an internal content management tool used by Luch Music and Gozar Records.

2. Information We Collect

LuchBot collects only the information necessary to operate the service, including phone numbers of authorized users, content files selected for posting, and captions generated through the tool.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used solely to facilitate the posting and scheduling of content to social media accounts managed by Luch Music and Gozar Records. We do not sell or share your information with third parties except as required to operate the service.

4. Third-Party Services

LuchBot connects to third-party services including TikTok, Instagram, Dropbox, and WhatsApp to perform its functions. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their use of your data.

5. Data Storage

Content metadata and scheduling information may be stored in Google Sheets for internal tracking purposes. No sensitive personal data is stored beyond what is necessary for the tool to function.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect information processed through LuchBot. Access is restricted to authorized team members only.

7. Your Rights

Authorized users may request removal of their data at any time by contacting team@gozarrecords.com.
